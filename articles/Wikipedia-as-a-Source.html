We occasionally use Wikipedia as a source; this is not best practice, and we acknowledge that.
So why do we still do it?
Well, for one thing, we don’t use Wikipedia as a source to *prove* anything, only as a basic source for basic information that doesn't need an in-depth source or as a starting point for further research.
In the latter situation, it is explicitly stated and a sentence such as “we recommend you use this Wikipedia article as a starting point for further research: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reliability_of_Wikipedia” A. Jack Sparrow
